Sourdough Starter

Essential in making Sourdough Bread!

Sourdough starter is the first step in making homemade bread the natural way!

Ingredients

  • Unbleached flour
  • Distilled water

Steps

  1. Get a container.
  2. Make sure you have plenty of space! It can grow three times its original size.
  3. Measure four oz of water, four oz of unbleached flour.
  4. Mix together.
  5. Put a towel or rag over it. Let sit.
  6. The next day, get rid of half then refeed it by giving it four oz of water and 4 oz of unbleached flour. Do this everyday. It has to be fed everyday! If you get tired of doing this I have ways you can permanently store.
